About Us



The idea of our company stemmed from the term "Saftaja," one of the oldest financial terms used throughout history to guarantee rights and facilitate transactions between parties. Although financial methods have evolved over centuries, we chose this ancient name to reflect the essence of our vision: combining authenticity and reliability on one hand, with innovation and technology on the other. From this perspective, Saftaja Information Technology Company strives to provide the best modern digital solutions that enhance trust and contribute to building a more efficient and transparent financial ecosystem. These solutions connect individuals and enterprises through advanced technological services that meet the aspirations of the financial sector and keep pace with future transformations.

Vision

To be pioneers in delivering smart and secure payment solutions that support digital transformation and financial inclusion, in alignment with Vision 2030 and the highest standards of security and international compliance.

Mission

To provide solutions for developing the fintech sector to elevate the payment market and enhance the operations of merchants using payment devices, in line with the developments in the financial sector and Vision 2030.

Objective

To provide fintech solutions that meet the payment needs of merchants and banks.
