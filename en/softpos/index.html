SoftPos

ST POS Is A Point of Sale (POS) hardware system (running on Android OS 10+)consists of dedicated physical devices and peripherals designed to process transactions. often used in retail stores, food trucks, restaurants, and other businesses requiring robust, reliable, and secure transaction processing.
STpay is a Soft POS (Point of Sale) application transforms smartphones, tablets, or other consumer-grade Android devices (android 10+) into payment terminals using software, eliminating the need for dedicated hardware. It leverages NFC (Near Field Communication), and card-reader dongles to process contactless payments, enabling businesses to accept credit/debit cards, mobile wallets (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay), and other digital payment methods directly on their existing devices.
This Product is under certification from SAMA
